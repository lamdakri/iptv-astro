# How to Set Up IPTV on Any Smart TV in 2026

Setting up IPTV on your Smart TV is easier than you think. This guide covers Samsung, LG, Sony, and Android TV setups with the most popular IPTV apps.

## What You Need

- An active IPTV subscription (like IPTV 4K World)
- Your M3U playlist URL or Xtream Codes credentials
- A stable internet connection (10 Mbps minimum for HD)

## Samsung Smart TV Setup

1. Go to the Samsung App Store
2. Search for "Smart IPTV" or "SS IPTV"
3. Install the app and open it
4. Note the MAC address displayed on screen
5. Upload your playlist via the Smart IPTV website

## LG Smart TV (WebOS) Setup

1. Open the LG Content Store
2. Search for "Smart IPTV" or "Nanomid"
3. Install and launch the application
4. Enter your M3U URL or upload via USB

## Android TV (Sony, TCL, Philips) Setup

1. Install "TiviMate" or "IPTV Smarters Pro" from the Google Play Store
2. Open the app and choose "Add Playlist"
3. Select "M3U URL" or "Xtream Codes API"
4. Enter your IPTV 4K World credentials
5. Enjoy 20,000+ channels in 4K quality

## Tips for Best Performance

- Use a wired Ethernet connection for stability
- Clear app cache regularly
- Update your TV firmware to the latest version
- Choose the right DNS settings for optimal streaming
