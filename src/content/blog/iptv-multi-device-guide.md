# How to Use IPTV on Multiple Devices in 2026

One of the biggest advantages of IPTV is watching on multiple devices at the same time. Here is everything you need to know.

## What is Multi-Device IPTV?

Multi-device IPTV allows you to stream IPTV on several devices simultaneously using a single subscription. This means:

- Watch sports in the living room while kids watch cartoons in the bedroom
- Stream on your TV while someone else watches on their phone
- Use IPTV at home and on the go

## How Many Devices Can You Use?

IPTV 4K World supports up to **4 simultaneous connections** on a single subscription. This covers most household needs.

## Tips for Multi-Device Streaming

1. **Internet Speed**: Each simultaneous stream needs ~10 Mbps for HD. For 4 streams, you need 40+ Mbps.
2. **Wired When Possible**: Use Ethernet for stationary devices like Smart TVs.
3. **Router Quality**: A good dual-band router handles multiple streams better.
4. **VPN on Router**: If using a VPN, install it on your router to protect all devices.
