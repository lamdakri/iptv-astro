# How to Record IPTV Live TV in 2026

One of the best features of IPTV is the ability to record live TV, pause, and rewind. Here is how.

## IPTV Recording Methods

### PVR (Personal Video Recorder)

PVR lets you schedule and record live TV programs to watch later. Most premium IPTV players support PVR.

### Timeshift

Timeshift allows you to pause live TV and resume watching later. Perfect for when you need a break during a live match.

### Catch-Up TV

Catch-up TV lets you watch programs that aired in the past 24-72 hours without recording them in advance. IPTV 4K World supports catch-up on major channels.

## Storage Requirements

- SD Recording: ~500 MB per hour
- HD Recording: ~2 GB per hour
- 4K Recording: ~7 GB per hour

Make sure you have enough storage space before scheduling long recordings.

## Tips for Best Recording Experience

1. Use a wired Ethernet connection for stable recording
2. Close background apps to free up resources
3. Schedule recordings in advance through the EPG
4. Regularly clean up old recordings
