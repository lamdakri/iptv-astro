# How to Watch IPTV on iPhone & iPad in 2025

Apple users can enjoy IPTV too. Here is how to set up IPTV on your iPhone, iPad, or Apple TV for the best streaming experience.

## Best IPTV Apps for iOS

### IPTVX — Best Premium iOS IPTV App

- Siri and AirPlay support
- EPG with schedule reminders

### GSE Smart IPTV
- Free with in-app purchases

- Built-in video player

### IPTV Smarters Pro
- Free to use
- Multi-platform support
- EPG integration
- Favorites management
- Video on demand support

## Step-by-Step iOS IPTV Setup

### On iPhone or iPad

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Search for **IPTVX** or **GSE Smart IPTV**
3. Download and install the app
4. Open the app and tap **Add Playlist**
5. Choose connection type:
   - **M3U URL**: Paste your playlist URL
   - **Xtream Codes**: Enter server, username, password
6. Tap **Connect** and start watching

### On Apple TV

1. Open the **App Store** on Apple TV
2. Install **IPTVX** or **IPTV Smarters Pro**
3. Open the app and add your playlist
4. Use the Siri Remote to navigate

## Tips for iOS IPTV

- **AirPlay**: Stream IPTV from your iPhone to Apple TV
- **Picture in Picture**: Watch IPTV while using other apps (iPad)
- **Background Audio**: Listen to IPTV audio with the screen off
- **iCloud Sync**: With IPTVX, your playlists sync across devices
